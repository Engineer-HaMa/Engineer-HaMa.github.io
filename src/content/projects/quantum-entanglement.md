title: Quantum Entanglement (EPR)
description: Investigation of the non-local correlations between quantum-mechanical particles — the EPR paradox and its implications.
importance: 2
category: quantum
---

The EPR paper (Einstein, Podolsky, Rosen 1935) argued that quantum mechanics is incomplete because it allows "spooky action at a distance." This project examines the foundations of quantum mechanics and the completeness question.
